Blinding light.

It is the first thing you see as you try to open your eyes.

Slowly the light dims to a tolerable level.

A faint high pitched noise rings in your ears.

As you gather your wits you realise you are in an empty but pure white room. There are no walls. There is nothing here. Nothing at all.

Why are YOU here?

Suddenly, all around you, you hear a maniacal howls of laughter.

You remember.

Your task. Your duty. Your quest.

You must destroy the Andy to save all of mankind.

You will have the last laugh.
