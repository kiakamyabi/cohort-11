The maniacal laughs get louder and closer to you by the second.

The light in the room begins to fade. Darkness begins to sweep behind you.

You hear some kind of mechanical sound. It is familar to you yet you aren't sure what it is.

The sound of a lever being pulled?

A hammer slamming down?

Someone's... foot crashing down?

A giant figure draped in shadows begins to march out of the all consuming darkness. You realise what the mechanical sound is.

It is Andy. Andy riding a treadmill.

His speed begins to pick up and the treadmill moves forward destroying everything in front of it.

Of course he can defy the laws of physics. It's Andy.

It's time to go. Now.

You sprint in the opposite direction. Andy starts to close the gap. One step at a time the treadmill picks up speed.

Fear begins to consume you. Is this the end? The quest has hardly begun!

In the distance you spot two doors. A way out.

Two signs protrude out of the ground with an arrow pointing at each door.

You calm yourself enough to read it. Despite the terrifying laughs from Andy edging ever closer to you.

Swansea. Bath Spa.

You can only choose one door to head towards.

What is your decision?

Swansea. Bath Spa.
